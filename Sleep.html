Mental Wellness

Better Sleep Tips

1. Set a Sleep Schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day to regulate your body clock.

2. Create a Relaxing Bedtime Routine

Take a warm bath, read a book, or meditate before sleeping to calm your mind.

3. Limit Screen Time

Avoid phones, tablets, and TVs at least 1 hour before bed to improve sleep quality.

4. Keep Your Room Cool and Dark

Maintain a comfortable sleeping environment with dim lighting and good airflow.

5. Avoid Caffeine and Heavy Meals

Refrain from caffeine and large meals at least 2-3 hours before bedtime.

6. Be Physically Active

Regular exercise during the day helps you fall asleep faster and improves sleep quality.

7. Use Relaxation Techniques

Try deep breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, or calming music before bed.

8. Seek Professional Help if Needed

If insomnia or sleep problems persist, consult a doctor or sleep specialist.

Treatment for Anxiety

1. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps you recognize and change negative thinking patterns and behaviors.

2. Mindfulness Meditation

Practicing mindfulness helps you stay grounded in the present and reduces anxious thoughts.

3. Breathing Exercises

Deep, controlled breathing can calm your nervous system and help manage acute anxiety.

4. Physical Activity

Regular exercise releases endorphins, which naturally relieve anxiety symptoms.

5. Limit Caffeine and Sugar

High caffeine and sugar intake can increase anxiety. Switch to healthier alternatives.

6. Stay Connected

Talking to family, friends, or support groups can ease stress and offer emotional support.

7. Sleep Hygiene

Getting adequate sleep improves your mental health and reduces anxiety levels.

8. Journaling

Write down your thoughts and triggers. It helps in identifying and managing anxiety.

9. Avoid Alcohol and Smoking

These substances may temporarily reduce anxiety but often worsen it in the long term.

10. Professional Help

If anxiety interferes with daily life, seek help from a licensed therapist or counselor.
