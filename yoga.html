Daily Yoga Routine

Perform these yoga poses daily for flexibility, strength, and relaxation.

Sukhasana (Easy Pose)

Hold: 1–2 minutes
Steps: Sit cross-legged, spine straight, close eyes and breathe deeply.

Cat-Cow Stretch

Reps: 5–10 rounds
Steps: Alternate between arching and rounding the spine while breathing.

Downward Facing Dog

Hold: 1 minute
Steps: Form an inverted V, hands and feet grounded, hips up.

Child’s Pose

Hold: 1–2 minutes
Steps: Kneel, sit back on heels, stretch arms forward, relax forehead to mat.

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Hold: 1 minute
Steps: Stand tall, feet together, arms by side, engage core, breathe steadily.

Standing Forward Bend

Hold: 1 minute
Steps: Hinge from hips, fold over legs, let the head hang.

Warrior I

Hold: 30 seconds each side
Steps: Lunge forward, back foot turned, arms up, chest forward.

Warrior II

Hold: 30 seconds each side
Steps: Arms extended, front knee bent, gaze over front hand.

Triangle Pose

Hold: 30 seconds each side
Steps: Extend arms, reach forward, tilt sideways, open chest.

Seated Forward Fold

Hold: 1 minute
Steps: Sit, legs forward, reach and fold over legs gently.

Bridge Pose

Hold: 1 minute
Steps: Lie on back, feet flat, lift hips, clasp hands under back.

Supine Twist

Hold: 30 seconds each side
Steps: Lie on back, twist bent knees to one side, arms open wide.

Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose

Hold: 3–5 minutes
Steps: Lie on back, lift legs against a wall, relax arms to sides.

Corpse Pose (Savasana)

Hold: 5–10 minutes
Steps: Lie on back, arms by sides, close eyes, breathe naturally, fully relax.
