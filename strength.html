Strength Training

Upper Body

Push-ups

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Start in a high plank position.
2. Keep your body straight.
3. Lower your body until elbows are at 90°.
4. Push back to the start position.

Dumbbell Chest Press

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps
Steps:
1. Lie flat on a bench.
2. Hold dumbbells above your chest.
3. Lower them slowly to chest level.
4. Press them back up.

Shoulder Press

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps
Steps:
1. Sit or stand with dumbbells at shoulder height.
2. Press dumbbells straight up.
3. Lower back down with control.

Dumbbell Rows

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Bend at hips with a straight back.
2. Hold dumbbells hanging down.
3. Pull dumbbells towards your torso.
4. Squeeze shoulder blades together, then lower.

Barbell Bent-Over Rows

Sets/Reps: 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps
Steps:
1. Hold barbell with overhand grip.
2. Slightly bend knees and lean forward at hips.
3. Pull barbell to lower chest.
4. Lower slowly with control.

Lat Pulldown

Sets/Reps: 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps
Steps:
1. Sit at the machine and grasp the bar wider than shoulders.
2. Pull the bar down to your upper chest.
3. Slowly release to start.

Face Pulls

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Steps:
1. Attach rope to high pulley.
2. Pull rope toward face, elbows high.
3. Squeeze shoulder blades.
4. Return slowly.

Pull-Ups

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 6-10 reps
Steps:
1. Grip bar wide.
2. Pull body up until chin clears bar.
3. Lower slowly to start.

Arms

Bicep Curls

3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Hold dumbbells at sides.
2. Curl them up while keeping elbows close.
3. Lower slowly.

Tricep Dips

3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Place hands on bench behind you.
2. Lower body by bending elbows.
3. Push back up to start.

Core

Plank

3 sets, 45 sec hold
Steps:
1. Lie face down, elbows under shoulders.
2. Raise body into straight line.
3. Hold position, keep core tight.

Plank

Place elbows on the floor and hold your body straight from head to toe for 30–60 seconds.

Crunches

Lie down, bend knees, and lift your upper body using your abdominal muscles. Do 15–20 reps.

Bicycle Crunch

1- Lie on your back and alternate elbow to opposite knee. Keep abs tight.

2 - Do 20 reps.

Mountain Climbers

Start in plank position, drive knees to chest alternately like running. Do for 30 seconds.

Russian Twist

Sit on the floor, lean back slightly, and twist torso side to side while holding your hands together or a weight.

2 - Do 20 reps

Lower Body

Squats

3 sets of 15 reps
Steps:
1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Lower hips until knees are at 90°.
3. Push through heels to rise.

Lunges

3 sets of 12 reps/leg
Steps:
1. Step forward with one leg.
2. Lower both knees to 90°.
3. Push back to start and switch leg.

Deadlifts (Bodyweight/Dumbbell)

3 sets of 10 reps
Steps:
1. Stand with feet hip-width apart.
2. Hinge at hips with straight back.
3. Lower and lift weight with control.

Back

Pull-Ups

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 6-10 reps
Steps:
1. Grip bar wide.
2. Pull body up until chin clears bar.
3. Lower slowly to start.

Push-ups

Sets/Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Start in a high plank position.
2. Keep your body straight.
3. Lower your body until elbows are at 90°.
4. Push back to the start position.

Superman

3 sets of 15 reps
Steps:
1. Lie face down on floor.
2. Extend arms and legs.
3. Lift arms, chest, and legs off the ground.
4. Hold briefly and lower.

Reverse Fly

3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Hold light weights.
2. Bend at hips and keep back straight.
3. Raise arms to sides.
4. Lower slowly.

Back Extensions

3 sets of 12 reps
Steps:
1. Lie on your stomach or back extension bench.
2. Cross arms or place behind head.
3. Lift upper body, keep hips down.
4. Lower back with control.
