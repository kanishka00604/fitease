Mental Wellness

Therapy Options for Better Mental Health

1. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT helps identify and change negative thought patterns. Itâ€™s widely used for anxiety, depression, and stress.

2. Talk Therapy

Also called psychotherapy, it involves speaking openly with a licensed professional to manage emotions and behavior.

3. Group Therapy

Group therapy brings together people with similar challenges. It provides support and different perspectives.

4. Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Combines therapy techniques with mindfulness meditation. Helps you stay present and reduce stress.

5. Art or Music Therapy

Uses creative expression to improve emotional well-being. Ideal for those who struggle with verbal communication.

6. Online Therapy

Access therapy from your phone or computer. Flexible and private, ideal for busy students and professionals.

7. Therapy for Students

On-campus counseling or online mental health platforms specifically for college students under pressure.

8. When Should You Seek Therapy?

Consider therapy if you feel overwhelmed, anxious, depressed, or unable to cope with daily life or studies.
