Building Healthy Relationships

1. Practice Active Listening

Listen to understand, not just to reply. Show empathy and interest in the other personâ€™s words.

2. Communicate Openly

Be honest and clear about your thoughts and feelings. Open communication builds trust.

3. Respect Boundaries

Every relationship needs boundaries. Respect others' space, opinions, and emotions.

4. Offer Support

Be available emotionally and mentally. Celebrate successes and stand by during hardships.

5. Spend Quality Time

Dedicate time to nurture your relationships through shared experiences and conversations.

6. Apologize and Forgive

Admit mistakes and forgive others to heal and grow stronger together.

7. Show Appreciation

Express gratitude and let others know you value them regularly.

8. Seek Help if Needed

If you're struggling in your relationships, talking to a counselor can help resolve conflicts.
