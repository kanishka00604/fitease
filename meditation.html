1. Sukhasana (Easy Pose)

Sit cross-legged with spine straight. Rest hands on knees. Ideal for beginners.

2. Padmasana (Lotus Pose)

Cross legs and place feet on opposite thighs. Enhances deep focus and stability.

3. Ardha Padmasana (Half Lotus)

One foot rests on the opposite thigh. Easier than full lotus, but still grounding.

4. Seiza (Kneeling Pose)

Kneel with buttocks on heels. Great for those with tight hips or knee issues.

5. Chair Meditation

Sit on a chair, feet flat, spine upright. Best for longer sessions or back support.
