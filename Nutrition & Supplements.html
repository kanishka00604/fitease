Nutrition & Supplements

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Include vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats in every meal to maintain energy and mood.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 7â€“8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration boosts metabolism and helps brain function.

3. Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Consume sources like fish, flax seeds, and walnuts. Omega-3s help reduce inflammation and anxiety.

4. Probiotics for Gut Health

Yogurt, kefir, and fermented foods improve digestion and gut-brain communication, supporting mental wellness.

5. Important Supplements

Consult a doctor for Vitamin D, B12, Magnesium, or Iron supplements if you're deficient to prevent fatigue and mood issues.
